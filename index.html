Better Bonds Relief Tokens

Better Bonds Entrepreneurship Inc is a non-governmental organization
dedicated to offering financial assistance to young entrepreneurs in Ghana.
Our mission extends to encompass various African countries as we progressively
expand our services. Our goal is to support aspiring young business owners who
possess the ambition to start a venture but face capital constraints. Through our
BBtoken program, we provide essential funding to empower these entrepreneurs
with the necessary capital. ​
